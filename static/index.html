While vouchers are designed to return unclaimed sats to the sender after the expiry period, this is not guaranteed. Transactions may fail, be delayed, or be lost due to technical issues.

The operator is not liable for any loss of funds, failed transactions, or damages arising from use of this service. You are solely responsible for safeguarding voucher links and redemption codes.

This service may be modified, interrupted, or permanently shut down at any time, without notice.

Voucher redemption includes a fee of 0.5% or 10 sats (whichever is greater). This fee is used to cover Lightning Network transaction costs.

Lightning routing fees are variable but are capped within this amount. If the full fee is required to complete the payment, no surplus is retained.

In most cases, actual network fees are lower. Any remaining sats are retained by the service and may be used at the operator’s discretion.
