About the Phone Number

The phone number is used only on your device to pre-fill the WhatsApp message when you share the voucher link. It is never sent to the server.

If you'd rather not enter a number, just leave it blank and tap Create Voucher. You can share the link any other way you like.

How it works

  1. Enter a phone number Create a voucher for the person you want to send sats to.
  2. Fund the voucher Pay the Lightning invoice with any Bitcoin wallet.
  3. Send the link Share the claim link via WhatsApp so they can redeem the sats.
  4. They claim the sats If they don’t claim in time, the sats return to your Lightning Address.
